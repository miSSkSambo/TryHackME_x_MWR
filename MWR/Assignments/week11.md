## 📖 Overview

This week focuses on understanding secure communication protocols, encryption, certificates, and professional penetration testing reporting.

Unlike previous weeks which focused heavily on exploitation, Week 11 introduces the defensive and documentation side of cybersecurity by covering:

- SSL & TLS
- HTTPS
- Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)
- Certificates & Certificate Authorities
- Secure communications
- Penetration testing reporting
- Technical documentation

The room explains how encrypted communication protects data and how cybersecurity professionals communicate findings through professional reports.
