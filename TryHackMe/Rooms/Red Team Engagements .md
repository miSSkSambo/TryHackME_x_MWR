## 📌 Overview

This room focuses on the **planning, structure, and documentation** of a red team engagement. It highlights how successful engagements rely not only on technical skills but also on **clear objectives, proper scoping, and detailed documentation**.

You learn how to transform client requirements into structured campaign plans and execute them effectively.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

- Understand components of a red team engagement
- Learn how to define scope and client objectives
- Understand Rules of Engagement (RoE)
- Learn campaign planning (CONOPS, Resource, Operations, Mission)
- Understand engagement documentation

## 🚀 Summary

This room shows that **successful red team engagements depend heavily on planning and documentation**, not just exploitation skills.

Key takeaways:
- Clear **objectives and scope** define the engagement
- **Rules of Engagement** ensure legal and safe operations
- Structured plans guide execution:
  - CONOPS → High-level strategy
  - Resource Plan → Logistics
  - Operations Plan → Execution details
  - Mission Plan → Operator-level actions

---

## ⚠️ Key Insight

A red team engagement is **not just hacking** — it is a **structured operation** that requires:
- Communication
- Planning
- Documentation
- Coordination
