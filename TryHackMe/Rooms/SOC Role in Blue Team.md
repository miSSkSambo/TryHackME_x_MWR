## 🚀 Summary

This room explains how cybersecurity teams are structured and how SOC fits into the **Blue Team ecosystem**.

Key takeaways:
- SOC is the **first line of defense**
- Blue Team focuses on **detecting and responding to threats**
- CIRT handles **critical incidents**
- CISO oversees the **entire security strategy**

---

## 📈 Career Path

1. **SOC L1 Analyst** → Alert triage  
2. **SOC L2 Analyst** → Deep investigations  
3. **SOC Engineer / Specialist Roles**  
4. **CIRT / Threat Hunting / Management**  
5. **CISO / Leadership Roles**

---

## ⚠️ Key Insight

SOC roles provide:
- Real-world experience with attacks
- Exposure to multiple tools and threats
- A strong foundation for any cybersecurity career

---

## 🔐 Internal SOC vs MSSP

| Feature        | Internal SOC | MSSP |
|----------------|------------|------|
| Environment    | Single company | Multiple clients |
| Pace           | Moderate | High-pressure |
| Tools          | Few, specialized | Many, diverse |
| Learning       | Slower but focused | Fast and broad |
