## 📌 Overview

This room focuses on the **planning, structure, and documentation** of a red team engagement. It explains how engagements are prepared from a business and technical perspective, including defining scope, rules of engagement (RoE), and building campaign plans.

The room emphasizes that successful red teaming is not just about hacking it’s about:
- Proper planning
- Clear communication
- Structured documentation

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

- Understand components of a red team engagement
- Learn how to define scope and objectives
- Understand Rules of Engagement (RoE)
- Learn campaign planning (Engagement, Operations, Mission, Remediation plans)
- Understand how to document engagements professionally

---

## 🧠 Key Concepts

- **Scope and Objectives**
- **Rules of Engagement (RoE)**
- **Campaign Planning**
- **CONOPS (Concept of Operations)**
- **Resource Plan**
- **Operations Plan**
- **Mission Plan**
- **Cyber Kill Chain alignment**

## 🚀 Summary

This room teaches that **red team success depends heavily on planning and documentation**, not just technical skills.

Key takeaways:
- Define clear **objectives and scope**
- Establish legal and operational boundaries via **Rules of Engagement**
- Use structured plans (CONOPS, Resource, Operations, Mission)
- Ensure communication and coordination across teams

A well-planned engagement ensures:
- Realistic attack simulation
- Minimal risk to the client
- Valuable insights for improving security posture
